## Targeting Notes

### Audience Targeting (Nationwide US)
- **Geography:** United States — no geo-restriction. Any US legal professional can attend.
- **Job titles:** Paralegal, Legal Assistant, Litigation Paralegal, Litigation Support Specialist, Legal Secretary
- **Interests:** National Association of Legal Assistants (NALA), National Federation of Paralegal Associations (NFPA), American Bar Association, Legal technology, Paralegal studies
- **Behaviors:** Engaged with legal job postings, continuing education interest
- **Age range:** 25-55
- **Exclude:** Attorneys, law students (this is paralegal-specific)

### Budget Recommendation
- **Daily budget:** $30-50/day for a single nationwide campaign (previously split across two city-level campaigns)
- **Duration:** 3-4 weeks before event (start ~March 28)
- **Phase 1 (weeks 1-2):** Broad targeting, all three hooks. Optimize for link clicks. $30/day.
- **Phase 2 (weeks 3-4):** Kill underperformers. Scale winning hook. Retarget site visitors. Increase to $50/day.
- **Total estimated spend:** $630-1,400 depending on performance scaling
- **Note:** Nationwide targeting gives Meta more room to optimize delivery vs. small-radius geo-targeting. Expect lower CPMs and better optimization.

### Format Specs
- **Video:** 9:16 (Stories/Reels), 1:1 (Feed). 15-30s. Captions required (80%+ watch on mute).
- **Carousel:** 1:1 (1080x1080px). 5 slides. Text-forward design — clean backgrounds, bold type, minimal imagery.

- `utm_medium=paid-social`
- `utm_campaign=workshop-online-apr25`

## Creative Direction

- **Tone:** Direct, practical, no-BS. These people are busy and skeptical. Don't oversell.
- **Visual style:** Clean, professional but not corporate. Show Zoom workshop screens, people engaged on laptops. Text overlays should be readable at mobile size.
- **What to avoid:** Generic "AI is the future" hype. Jargon. Anything that looks like a tech bro webinar pitch. The word "webinar" (this is a workshop — there's a difference).
- **Key differentiator:** This is paralegal-specific, not "AI for lawyers." That distinction matters to the audience.
- **Online advantage to emphasize:** "Join from anywhere" — removes the barrier of travel. But keep the scarcity real: 25 seats, not 500.
