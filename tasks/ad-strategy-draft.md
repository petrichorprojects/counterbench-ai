# CounterbenchAI Ad Strategy — DRAFT

**Status:** DRAFT — For Philipp's review, not final

**Target audience:** Paralegals + Law firm managers

---

## 1. Product Positioning (What We're Actually Selling)

CounterbenchAI is a **curated legal AI directory and resource platform**:
- 275+ reviewed legal AI tools
- 800+ ready-to-use prompts for legal tasks
- 30+ workflow skills and 10+ practice playbooks
- Interactive tools (contract QA planner, source finder, LawGlance planner)
- Collections organized by practice area

**Core value prop for ads:** "Find the right AI tool for your legal workflow in seconds — not hours of Googling."

---

## 2. Target Audience Segments

### Segment A: Paralegals (Primary)
- **Pain:** Overwhelmed with manual tasks, curious about AI but don't know where to start
- **Intent signals:** Searching for AI tools, prompts for legal drafting, workflow efficiency
- **Platforms:** Google Search, Meta (Facebook groups for paralegals)
- **Messaging angle:** "Work faster, not harder — 800+ AI prompts built for your workflow"

### Segment B: Law Firm Managers / Partners (Secondary)
- **Pain:** Staffing shortages, billing pressure, need to modernize without risk
- **Intent signals:** Searching for legal tech solutions, paralegal productivity, AI adoption
- **Platforms:** Google Search, LinkedIn (future phase)
- **Messaging angle:** "Give your team AI tools vetted for legal work — no experimentation needed"

---

## 3. Campaign Types

### 3.1 Google Search — Bottom-of-Funnel (Priority 1)

**Why first:** Highest intent. People actively searching for legal AI tools convert better than cold audiences.

**Keyword themes:**

| Theme | Example Keywords | Intent Level |
|-------|-----------------|--------------|
| Legal AI tools | legal ai tools, ai tools for law firms, best ai for legal work | High |
| Paralegal productivity | paralegal ai tools, ai for paralegals, paralegal workflow tools | High |
| Legal prompts/templates | legal ai prompts, chatgpt prompts for lawyers, ai prompts for legal drafting | High |
| Legal tech discovery | legal technology directory, legal tech tools list, ai legal tech comparison | High |
| Specific task searches | ai contract review tool, ai legal research tool, ai deposition summary | High |
| Pain-point searches | paralegal overwhelmed, too many legal ai tools, which ai for legal work | Medium |

**Ad structure:**
- 3-5 tightly themed ad groups (one per keyword theme above)
- Responsive search ads with 15 headlines / 4 descriptions per ad group
- Sitelink extensions to: Tools directory, Prompts library, Practice playbooks, Free resources

**Landing pages:** Direct to relevant collection pages (e.g., /tools for general, specific practice area pages for niche keywords)

### 3.2 Meta (Facebook/Instagram) — Top-of-Funnel Awareness (Priority 2)

**Why second:** Paralegals are active in Facebook groups and on Instagram. Good for awareness and list building before they're actively searching.

**Campaign types:**

1. **Awareness/Traffic campaigns**
   - Carousel ads showcasing "5 AI tools every paralegal should know"
   - Short video ads: "Still doing [manual task]? There's an AI tool for that."
   - Target: Paralegals (job title), legal assistant, law firm employee

2. **Lead generation campaigns**
   - Free resource download: "Top 50 AI Prompts for Paralegals" (PDF)
   - Webinar/guide signup: "How to Pick the Right Legal AI Tool"
   - Builds email list for nurture sequences

3. **Retargeting campaigns**
   - Site visitors who browsed tools but didn't sign up
   - People who started but didn't finish a resource download

**Audience targeting:**
- Job titles: Paralegal, Legal Assistant, Litigation Paralegal, Office Manager (law firm)
- Interests: Legal technology, American Bar Association, Paralegal associations
- Lookalike audiences from site visitors (once pixel has data)

## 4. Budget Tiers

### Tier 1: Validation ($500-$1,000/month)
- **Goal:** Prove demand, find winning keywords, validate messaging
- **Allocation:**
  - Google Search: $400-$800 (80%)
  - Meta awareness: $100-$200 (20%)
- **Duration:** 4-6 weeks minimum for statistical significance
- **Success criteria:** CPC under $5, CTR above 3%, identify 2-3 winning keyword themes
- **When to move up:** Consistent conversions at acceptable CPA

### Tier 2: Growth ($1,500-$3,000/month)
- **Goal:** Scale winning campaigns, add retargeting, test Meta lead gen
- **Allocation:**
  - Google Search: $900-$1,800 (60%)
  - Meta (awareness + retargeting): $450-$900 (30%)
  - Meta lead gen: $150-$300 (10%)
- **Success criteria:** CPA under $15, growing email list, improving Quality Scores

### Tier 3: Scale ($5,000-$10,000/month)
- **Goal:** Dominate key search terms, full-funnel Meta, test LinkedIn
- **Allocation:**
  - Google Search: $2,500-$5,000 (50%)
  - Meta full-funnel: $1,500-$3,000 (30%)
  - LinkedIn (law firm managers): $500-$1,000 (10%)
  - Testing budget (new channels/creatives): $500-$1,000 (10%)
- **Success criteria:** Branded search volume growing, CAC payback under 3 months

## 6. Creative Direction

### Google Search Ads
- **Headlines:** Lead with specificity and numbers ("275+ Legal AI Tools Reviewed", "800+ AI Prompts for Legal Work", "Free Legal AI Directory")
- **Descriptions:** Address pain directly ("Stop Googling for AI tools. We reviewed 275+ so you don't have to.")
- **CTAs:** "Browse Free", "Find Your Tool", "Get Started Free"

### Meta Ads
- **Visual style:** Clean, professional — avoid "techy" stock photos. Use screenshots of the actual directory and tool cards.
- **Copy tone:** Direct, practical — match the content voice (authoritative, not corporate). Paralegals are skeptical; be specific.
- **Formats to test:** Static image, carousel (tool showcases), short video (< 30s problem/solution)

---

## 7. Phased Rollout

### Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4): Google Search Only
- Set up conversion tracking in GTM
- Launch 3 ad groups around highest-intent keyword themes
- Establish baseline CPC, CTR, CPA
- Build retargeting audiences via pixel

### Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8): Add Meta
- Launch awareness campaigns targeting paralegals
- Start retargeting site visitors
- Test 2-3 ad creatives per audience
- Begin lead gen with free resource offer

### Phase 3 (Weeks 9-12): Optimize + Expand
- Kill underperforming keywords and ads
- Scale winners with increased budget
- Test lookalike audiences on Meta
- Evaluate LinkedIn for law firm manager segment
