## Sequence Rules

1. **If attendee books Assessment at any point**: Remove from remaining sequence. Move to Assessment follow-up sequence (separate — not covered here).
2. **If attendee clicks Assessment link but doesn't book**: Send a 1-line follow-up 24 hours later: "Saw you checked out the Assessment booking page — any questions I can answer before you pick a time?"
3. **If attendee opens zero emails after Day 0**: Drop from sequence after Day 7. They're not interested — don't burn the relationship.
4. **All emails come from Philipp personally** — not "VerdictOps Team" or a noreply address. The workshop built trust with Philipp. The sequence extends it.
5. **Reply handling**: Any reply to any email in the sequence triggers a manual follow-up from Philipp within 24 hours. These are warm leads. Don't automate replies.
