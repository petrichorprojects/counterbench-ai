Questions We Hear

How is this different from hiring a fractional CTO?

A fractional CTO manages your technology infrastructure. We build your AI strategy. Most CTOs are generalists — they've never evaluated plaintiff-specific AI tools or tracked carrier AI systems. We do nothing else.

Do you get paid by AI vendors?

No. Zero commissions. Zero partnerships. Zero affiliate revenue. If we recommend EvenUp over Eve for your firm, it's because the data supports it for your case mix — not because they're paying us.

My firm is only 5 attorneys. Is AI worth it?

A 5-attorney firm that automates medical chronologies gains back 1,000+ paralegal hours per year. AI scales down, not just up. The question isn't firm size — it's case volume.

Can't I just figure this out from vendor demos?

You can. But every vendor demo is a sales presentation. They'll show you their best cases and hide their weaknesses. We show you both. And we evaluate them against each other, which no vendor will do for you.

What if I'm not ready to commit to a monthly retainer?

Start with the free AI Readiness Diagnostic. Subscribe to The Counterbench newsletter. When you're ready, the advisory is here. No pressure.

How long do clients typically stay?

The AI landscape changes quarterly. Most clients stay 6-12+ months because the advisory remains relevant as new tools launch, carriers upgrade, and their firm's needs evolve. But engagements are month-to-month — no lock-in.
