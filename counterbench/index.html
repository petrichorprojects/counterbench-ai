The Arms Race

The defense has been weaponizing AI for decades. The plaintiff bar is just waking up.

Colossus, ClaimIQ, and Claims Outcome Advisor have processed billions in claims. These systems assign severity points, anchor settlement ranges, and train adjusters to systematically minimize your demands.

In 2025, agentic AI pilots began rolling out across major carriers. The next generation won't just score claims — it will negotiate them autonomously.

Meanwhile, $700 million flooded into plaintiff AI tools — EvenUp, Eve, Supio — but 81% of PI firms haven't adopted a single one. The asymmetry is growing. Every month without a strategy is a month the gap compounds.
