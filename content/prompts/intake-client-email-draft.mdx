## Prompt

Draft an intake acknowledgement email. This is not legal advice.

Client: {{client_name}}
Tone: {{tone}}

Matter summary (internal, 2-4 sentences):
{{matter_summary}}

Requested documents:
{{requested_documents}}

Next steps:
{{next_steps}}

Requirements:
- Clear, concise, professional.
- Ask for documents and confirm preferred deadline.
- Include a confidentiality reminder (do not email sensitive items unless a secure channel is used).
