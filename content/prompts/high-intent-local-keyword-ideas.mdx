## Prompt

List 20 high-intent local keywords for a `{{service}}` in `{{city}}`, `{{state}}` that indicate a customer is ready to buy NOW.

Instantly, you will have a list of "near me" and "emergency" keywords that actually convert.

List **20** high-intent local keywords for:

- Service: `{{service}}`
- Location: `{{city}}`, `{{state}}`

### Rules

- These must indicate the searcher is ready to buy **now**.
- Include “near me” and urgency variants, but don’t repeat trivial permutations.
- If the service is regulated (e.g., legal/medical), avoid misleading claims.

### Output format

Return:

1) **Emergency / Urgent intent** (at least 6)
2) **Near-me / local intent** (at least 6)
3) **Price / consult intent** (at least 4)
4) **Problem-specific intent** (at least 4)

Then add:

- **Negative keywords to consider** (5–15) to avoid low-intent traffic for this service.
