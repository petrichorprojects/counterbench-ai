## Prompt

You are a paralegal assistant. Convert the following intake notes into a structured summary that a supervising attorney can review quickly.

### Intake notes

Paste notes here.

### Output format

- Parties
- Facts (bullet points)
- Timeline (date ordered)
- Injuries and treatment
- Damages (economic, non-economic)
- Liability theories
- Open questions (what we must ask next)
- Next steps (documents to request, deadlines)
