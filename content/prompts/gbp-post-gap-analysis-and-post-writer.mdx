## Prompt

Analyze these competitor GBP posts by opening their GBP in Chrome. Identify their keyword gaps, then write 10 high-impact posts for my business in `{{city}}` that include local landmarks and a hard "Call Now" CTA.

Codex does the heavy lifting while I take the #1 spot.

You are optimizing my **Google Business Profile** content cadence to beat local competitors.

City: `{{city}}`, `{{state}}`
Business: `{{business_name}}`
Call-now phone: `{{phone}}`

Competitors (provide GBP links if possible; otherwise use their exact business names and find their GBP):
- `{{competitor_gbp_1}}`
- `{{competitor_gbp_2}}`
- `{{competitor_gbp_3}}`

### Step 1 — Open competitor GBPs and audit posts

In Chrome:

1) Open each competitor GBP.
2) Review their **most recent posts** (if posts are not visible, say so and stop).

For each competitor, output a table with:

- Post date (if visible)
- Post type (Offer / Update / Event / Product / Other)
- Primary topic
- Primary keywords/phrases used (exact phrases where possible)
- CTA used (Call now / Learn more / Book / etc.)
- Trust signals referenced (reviews, awards, “X years”, results, etc.)
- Local anchors used (neighborhoods, landmarks, local terms)

### Step 2 — Identify gaps

Output:

1) **Keyword gaps** (phrases they don’t use that a buyer would search; high intent only)
2) **Offer gaps** (consultation offers, urgency, guarantees, financing, etc.)
3) **Trust gaps** (proof points they aren’t leveraging that we can)
4) **Local relevance gaps** (neighborhood/landmark anchors they ignore)

### Step 3 — Write 10 high-impact GBP posts (ready to paste)

Write **10** posts for `{{business_name}}` in `{{city}}` that:

- Use at least **1 local landmark or neighborhood** each (rotate; don’t repeat the same one)
- Include **one** high-intent keyword phrase per post (natural, not spammy)
- Include a **hard CTA**: “Call now at `{{phone}}`.”
- Avoid compliance-risky claims (no “guaranteed results”, no “best”, no unverifiable superlatives)

Format each post exactly like this:

1) **Post Title:** …
2) **Post Body:** 70–130 words.
3) **Primary Keyword:** …
4) **Local Anchor:** …
5) **CTA:** Call now at `{{phone}}`.

Also output a 1-line **posting schedule** recommendation (e.g., 3/week) based on competitor cadence.
