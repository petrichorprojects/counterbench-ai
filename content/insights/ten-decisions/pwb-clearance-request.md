## Recording the answers

When clearance comes back, update in this order:

1. Each episode's status line: `**Status:** SHIP-READY. Rule cleared by PWB [date].`
2. The `README.md` clearance table
3. Any corrected thresholds in **both** the episode file and `compiled-lead-magnet.md`. The two drift easily and the compiled version is what becomes the PDF.

If a rule comes back wrong rather than corrected, the episode does not publish. The slot moves and the series runs 9 weeks. Publishing an uncleared constructed rule is the one failure mode this whole protocol exists to prevent.

## Why this request is scoped this way

Asking a practicing attorney to review 9 posts is a favor with a real time cost, and the response rate on that request is low. Asking for right/wrong/corrected-number on 9 blockquotes is a 20-minute task.

The 4 flagged rules are the ones where being wrong costs the most: 2 carry invented numbers, 1 touches withdrawal ethics, and 1 makes a clinical distinction I inferred rather than knew. Naming them concentrates the attention where the risk is.
