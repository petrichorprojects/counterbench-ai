## What this is

A 10-week LinkedIn series naming the 10 decisions that repeat on every PI file, and writing each one as a rule a paralegal can execute without interrupting a partner.

The series is the asset. Individual posts are the delivery mechanism.

## Why it exists

Three things happen at once:

1. **Weekly presence** on the CounterbenchAI page with a format the audience recognizes by week 3.
2. **A lead magnet that earns its download.** The compiled PDF converts in week 11 because 10 weeks of published proof preceded it.
3. **A named first deliverable** for the Paralegal Teams retainer. "We document your ten decisions in the first 30 days" is a concrete onboarding artifact instead of a vague promise.

Same work, three uses. That's the multiplier.

## PWB clearance protocol

Every episode from 01 on ships a **constructed rule**. The rules are a synthesis of PI practice, not citations. That's what makes them useful and it's also the entire risk: a reader tests the rule against their own files in about 4 seconds, and a wrong rule in front of PI attorneys costs more than the post earns.

Ep01 established the pattern. A practicing PI attorney at PWB reviewed the rule and cleared it, 2026-07-22.

**Standing rule: constructed and cleared is publishable. Constructed and uncleared is not.**

Send Ep02-10 to PWB as one batch. Each file carries its rule in a single blockquote, so the review is 9 blockquotes rather than 9 posts. Record the clearance date in the file's status line and in the table above when it comes back.

## Format contract (every episode)

Each post ships the actual rule. Withholding the rule and teasing the download kills the series. The rule is the proof that CounterbenchAI knows the work.

Structure:
1. Claim on line 1.
2. Mechanism: why this decision moves money or risk.
3. Evidence: the field observation, in position 2 or later. Never the opener.
4. **The rule.** Blockquoted. Conditions, exceptions, and the call.
5. Where it sits in the 10.
6. Direct DM CTA.

## First-hour protocol

1. Zero links in post body. First comment only if unavoidable.
2. Phil replies to every comment inside 60 minutes, each reply adding something.
3. Pre-send to 3-5 PI attorneys Phil actually knows: "curious if this matches your firm." Genuine ask.
4. No edits for 90 minutes.

## Voice compliance

Governed by `counterbench-voice.md` + `_ops/templates/banned-phrases.md`.

Hard checks before any episode ships:
- [ ] Zero em dashes
- [ ] Zero negation-then-correction ("Not X. Y." / "This isn't X, it's Y") - FATAL, auto-fail
- [ ] Contractions present
- [ ] Numbers as digits
- [ ] No "nobody" / "most people don't realize" constructions
- [ ] No buzzwords (AI-powered, intelligent, seamless, transform, leverage, unlock)
- [ ] No comment-bait CTA. Direct DM or newsletter only.
- [ ] Paragraph lengths vary. Uniform rhythm is a structural AI tell.
- [ ] Named legal risk present (malpractice, bar complaint, missed deadline, reserve)
