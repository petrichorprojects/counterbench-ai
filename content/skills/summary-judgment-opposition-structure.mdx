## Steps

1. List elements and the disputed facts for each.
2. Build a fact table: Defense claim | Your dispute | Evidence cite.
3. Outline legal standard and apply to facts.
4. Conclude with what must go to a jury.

## Output Template

```text
Opposition Outline

- Standard of review
- Statement of facts (with cites)
- Disputed issues table
- Argument by element
- Conclusion
```
