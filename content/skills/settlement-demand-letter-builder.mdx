## Steps

1. Open with a crisp liability narrative and why the defense loses.
2. Summarize injuries and treatment with key dates.
3. Quantify specials and wage loss; mention future care.
4. Make a specific demand with a response deadline.

## Copy/Paste Prompt

```text
You are drafting a plaintiff-side settlement demand letter.

Inputs:
- Adjuster + carrier: [ ]
- Insured: [ ]
- Claim #: [ ]
- Liability narrative: [PASTE]
- Injuries + treatment summary: [PASTE]
- Medical bills to date: $[ ]
- Wage loss: $[ ]
- Future care/permanency: [PASTE/UNKNOWN]
- Demand amount: $[ ]
- Deadline: [DATE]

Output:
- Professional demand letter (1.5–2 pages)
- Sections: Liability, Injuries/Treatment, Damages, Demand + deadline
- Tone: firm, credible, not inflammatory
- Include a short exhibit list at the end

Do not invent facts. If something is missing, insert [NEEDED: ...].
```

## Quality Checks
- Reads like a story with dates and support.
- Demand is stated once, clearly, with deadline.
- No extra adjectives; all claims tie to facts.
