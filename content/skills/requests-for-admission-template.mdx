## Steps

1. Pick facts that should be uncontested (control, dates, authenticity).
2. Draft admissions that are simple and hard to dodge.
3. Attach a supporting exhibit list for follow-up.

## Copy/Paste Prompt

```text
Draft requests for admission for a PI case.

Inputs:
- Case theory: [ ]
- Facts to lock in: [LIST]
- Exhibits available: [LIST]

Output:
- 15–25 RFAs grouped by theme
- Include authenticity/admissibility starters
- Avoid legal conclusions unless clearly allowed
```
