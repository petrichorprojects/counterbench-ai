## Steps

1. Establish what the expert must prove (and what they must avoid).
2. Identify bias signals: repeat work, same firms/carriers, publication gaps.
3. Extract repeat opinions and pressure points.
4. Produce cross themes and exhibit list.

## Output Template

```text
Expert Vetting Summary

- Expert: [ ]
- Specialty: [ ]
- Role: [Defense/Plaintiff]

Bias/Repeat-Player Signals
- …

Likely Opinions (and where they show up)
- …

Credibility vulnerabilities
- …

Cross themes (3)
1) …
2) …
3) …

Exhibits to gather
- Prior reports
- Prior transcripts
- Billing/retention
```

## Quality Checks
- Separates verified facts from inference.
- Produces 3 tight cross themes, not 30 questions.
