## Steps

1. List medical specials + sources.
2. Compute wage loss with explicit dates and rate.
3. Summarize future care items and what supports them.
4. Draft non-economic narrative tied to facts.
5. Flag liens/subrogation and unknowns.

## Output Template

```text
Damages Snapshot

Economic
- Medical bills to date: $[ ] (Source: [ ])
- Lost wages: $[ ] (Assumptions: [ ])
- Out-of-pocket: $[ ]
- Future care: $[ ] (Support: [ ])

Non-Economic (facts-based)
- Pain level + duration:
- ADL limitations:
- Sleep/household/work impact:

Liens/Subrogation
- Known liens: [ ]
- Unknowns to confirm: [ ]

Conservative valuation notes (what drives up/down)
- Up drivers: …
- Down drivers: …
```

## Quality Checks
- Every number has a source or an assumption.
- Non-economic narrative references concrete events (missed work, PT sessions, etc.).
- Lien risks called out.
