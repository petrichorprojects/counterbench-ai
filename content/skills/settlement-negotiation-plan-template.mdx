## Steps

1. Define your opening, target, and floor (with reasons).
2. List 3 credibility points (proof) and 3 emotional points (impact).
3. Pre-plan concessions: what you can trade vs cannot.
4. Define stall responses and escalation options.

## Output Template

```text
Negotiation Plan
- Opening: $[ ] (why)
- Target: $[ ] (why)
- Floor: $[ ] (why)

Credibility points (proof):
- …
Impact points (human):
- …

Concession ladder (Round 1/2/3):
- …

If they stall: scripts + next move
```
