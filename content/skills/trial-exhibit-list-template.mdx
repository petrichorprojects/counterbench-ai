## Steps

1. Group exhibits by theme (liability, damages, impeachment).
2. For each exhibit, note what it proves and who lays foundation.
3. Anticipate likely objections and responses.

## Output Template

```text
Exhibit | Description | Purpose (what it proves) | Foundation witness | Objections to anticipate
```
