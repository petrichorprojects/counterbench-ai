## Steps

1. Confirm correct parties, names, and service addresses.
2. Confirm venue and jurisdiction allegations.
3. State facts with dates and location (no fluff).
4. Plead each cause of action with elements in mind.
5. Plead damages categories (economic + non-economic) and any special relief.
6. Attach verifications/notices required by local rules (if any).
