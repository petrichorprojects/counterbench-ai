"Why Now" Triggers

These signals mean a firm is ready to buy today. Check before you reach out — it gives you a specific reason to call beyond the cold cadence.

  • New negative review posted in last 30 days — and no response from the firm. Search "[Firm Name] Google reviews" before calling. Name the specific review.
  • Paralegal job posting on Indeed or LinkedIn — they're understaffed and know it. This is an open admission of the problem we solve.
  • New attorney just joined the firm — growth moment. Workflows are being rebuilt. Great time to introduce infrastructure.
  • Managing partner changed in last 6 months — new leadership often moves fast on operational fixes.
  • High review volume, low average rating — visible problem, easy to quantify. "You've got 23 reviews and 3.8 stars. Three unanswered 1-stars are pulling you down."
  • Competitor firm in same market is responding to reviews — show them side by side. Nothing motivates action like a direct comparison.
